Lorena Garcia

Nonprofit leader, 36

Garcia, 36, was the first candidate to officially announce on Nov. 27, 2018, less than a month after the election. Garcia has spent her professional career working for different charities and is currently the executive director of the Colorado Statewide Parent Coalition. “Throughout my career, I have had direct experience working in the field with people who are impacted by federal policies,” Garcia told The Denver Post. “I’ve sat with them while they cried when things didn’t pass or didn’t work out right. … I believe we can do much better as a country, starting with Colorado.” • Read Lorena Garcia coverage →
