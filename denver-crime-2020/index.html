A rise in Denver property crime

Property crimes rose across the city in 2020, but the extent of the rise varied neighborhood to neighborhod. Property crimes include burglary, larceny, auto theft, theft from vehicles and arson. Auraria and DIA were left off the list because they are not primarily residential neighborhoods.
