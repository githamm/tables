Racial disparity among district court judges

White judges are over-represented in most of Colorado's 22 judicial districts. The graphic shows the racial breakdown of current district judges and what the breakdown would look like if it matched the population that lives within the districts. If fractions of different racial populations were enough to "equal" one judge, it is categorized as "Non-white." County judges are not included in the analysis. Source: Denver Post research, Colorado Judicial Branch, 2018 5-year American Community Survey
