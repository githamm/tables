Colorado donors to the Republican Governors Association and Democratic Governors Association

The Republican Governors Association and the Democratic Governors Association — advocacy groups called 527 organizations that can raise unlimited money from donors and report their contributions and expenditures to the IRS — are putting money into the Colorado governor's race. This database shows the companies and individuals from Colorado who have donated from Jan. 1, 2017, through June 30, 2018. Note: Individuals who donated less than $300 are listed with the name "Individual." Source: Internal Revenue Service
